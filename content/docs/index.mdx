Vizzor is a crypto intelligence stack, not a chat wrapper. The **Predictor** turns six independent signal families into calibrated directional forecasts with tracked win-rates. The **Scanner** runs structural security audits on any token. The **Whale Terminal** renders per-token smart-money flow. **Forensics** reconstructs the on-chain graph the rest of the surface reads from. Everything is callable from Telegram, the CLI, the REST API, the web dashboard, and a multi-LLM chat that knows how to call them as tools.

This documentation is the operator's reference — every primitive, every command, every knob.

## What makes Vizzor different

- **Not a chart tool** — a prediction engine that fuses six independent signal types into one composite forecast, with tracked win-rate on every horizon.
- **Not a news aggregator** — sentiment, derivatives positioning, on-chain flow, and TA are processed simultaneously and contribute to the same emission.
- **Not a chatbot wrapper** — every prediction is backed by real market data from 15 exchanges, with a traceable trigger snapshot showing which signal families fired.
- **Not a black box** — abstention is a feature: the engine refuses to predict under manipulation patterns, low data coverage, or pre-event windows. `/diagnose` shows exactly why.
- **Works with everything** — BTC, ETH, SOL, meme coins, new DEX launches, tokens by contract address. If it has a price on one of the 11 supported chains, Vizzor can predict it.
