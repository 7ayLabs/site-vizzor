# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-06._

This is the operator-facing source of truth for what data Vizzor's
website collects, why, how long we keep it, and what you can do about
it. The end-user-facing page lives at
[`/legal/privacy`](https://vizzor.ai/legal/privacy).

The site is operated by 7ayLabs ("we", "us"). For the purposes of
GDPR Vizzor's website is the **data controller** for the items below.

---

## What we collect

| Category | Item | Source | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Authentication | Wallet address (public Solana base58 string) | User signs SIWS challenge | Identify the wallet for subscription state. |
| Authentication | Auth-session token | Generated by us on SIWS verify; raw value lives only in your browser cookie | Maintain a 24h browser session. **We store SHA-256(token), not the raw token.** |
| Subscription state | Wallet → tier × cadence × expiry mapping | Watcher confirms an on-chain payment | Decide if `/predict` and the Telegram bot grant access. |
| Telegram binding | Telegram user ID | Bot calls `/api/grants/.../redeem` after you click the deep link | Bind your subscription to your Telegram account so the bot recognizes you in chat. |
| Rate limit | Hashed client IP | HMAC-SHA-256 of the connecting IP with a server-side salt | Defend against enumeration and brute-force; raw IP is never persisted. |
| Audit | Hashed subject + outcome + hashed IP + hashed UA prefix | Every bot-route PII read / write | Detect bot-secret leaks and unauthorized lookups during incident response. |
| Cryptographic forensics | SIWS signature (when you bind a wallet to Telegram) | Wallet signature on a structured message | Prove the binding was authorized in case of dispute. |
| Operational | Container logs (stderr + stdout) | Node process | Detect crashes; wallet addresses are truncated to first-4 / last-4 before any log statement. |

## What we do not collect

- Email addresses, real names, postal addresses, phone numbers.
- Browser fingerprints, device IDs, advertising IDs.
- Analytics cookies (no Plausible, PostHog, Google Analytics,
  Vercel Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
- Behavioral tracking across sessions.
- Marketing pixels.

We don't have a marketing department. The site has no third-party
script tags except for the Solana wallet adapter (which runs on the
user's device, not against our servers).

## Cookies we set

| Name | Purpose | TTL | Flags |
|---|---|---|---|
| `vizzor.siws.nonce` | One-time SIWS nonce | 5 min | `HttpOnly; SameSite=Strict; Secure` (prod) |
| `vizzor.auth` | Auth session token (raw value never persists server-side; we store its SHA-256 hash) | 24 hours | `HttpOnly; SameSite=Lax; Secure` (prod) |

We do not set tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party
cookies of any kind.

## Retention windows

| Data | Window | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| Failed / expired payment sessions | 30 days | Daily retention sweep |
| Confirmed payment sessions | 365 days | Daily retention sweep (tax / audit retention) |
| Grants (after expiry) | 90 days | Daily retention sweep |
| Wallet-link challenges | 7 days | Daily retention sweep |
| Idempotency keys | 7 days | Daily retention sweep |
| Rate-limit buckets | 1 day | Daily retention sweep |
| Audit log | 365 days | Daily retention sweep |
| Signature replay cache | ~4 thousand most-recent signatures | Size-capped eviction |

Override windows via env (`VIZZOR_RETENTION_DAYS_*`). Sweep runs at
03:42 UTC daily via the GitHub Actions cron at
[`.github/workflows/retention-sweep.yml`](../../.github/workflows/retention-sweep.yml).

## Right to erasure (GDPR Article 17 / CCPA)

Sign in with the wallet whose data you want deleted, then POST to
`/api/account/delete`. The handler:

1. DELETEs your row from `wallet_links` (binding removed).
2. NULLs `subscriptions.telegram_user_id` and replaces
   `subscriptions.wallet_address` with a tombstone hash.
3. Scrubs `payment_sessions.payer_address` for non-confirmed rows.
4. DELETEs every active auth session for the wallet.
5. Logs the request to the audit log with the hashed subject.

Confirmed payment session records are **retained for 1 year** for tax
and audit purposes. After 1 year the daily sweep removes them.

## Third-party data flows

| Surface | What leaks | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Solana RPC provider | Your IP, when your browser / our server calls the RPC | Use a private RPC; the public fallback rate-limits per IP. Override via `SOLANA_RPC_URL_MAINNET`. |
| `cdn.jsdelivr.net` (cryptocurrency icons) | Your IP per icon fetch | Mirror to `public/coins/` per the local override pattern in `lib/coin-meta.ts`. |
| `coin-images.coingecko.com` (for the three brand-current coin icons we host locally) | None — fetched server-side at build by the maintainer | n/a |
| Sentry (if `SENTRY_DSN` is configured by the operator) | Stack traces, request metadata. **Cookies, signatures, and bot tokens are redacted by `beforeSend` before send.** | Operator can leave `SENTRY_DSN` unset to disable entirely. |

## Legal basis (GDPR)

- Wallet ↔ Telegram binding: **consent** (you explicitly signed the
  SIWS challenge and clicked the bot deep-link).
- Subscription state: **performance of a contract** (you paid for
  access; we owe you the access).
- Rate-limit hashing: **legitimate interest** (defend the service
  against abuse). IPs are never persisted raw.
- Audit log: **legal obligation** (we need to be able to demonstrate
  what bot-secret-authenticated calls happened during an incident).
